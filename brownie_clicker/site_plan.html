Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide an enjoyable pastime game. The game resembles the playstyle of cookie clicker. In a real-world setting, the game would make money through ad revenue.

Audience

The intended audience consists of school children and middle-aged unemployed parents. This target audience would be most likely to spend time on an online game, and would be most likely to understand the concept and strategy of such a game.

Dynamic elements

The game consists of one large "brownie" - which when clicked, increases the overall total of the player. The player aims to maximize the number of brownies they have produced. As such, dynamic elements include buttons to purchase more workers to produce brownies, which will add a small representation of the worker to the page, and subtract the required number of brownies from the total. Additional dynamic elements consist of a "news bar" and "settings menu". The news bar displays funny snippets of news, related to the current number of produced brownies. The settings menu has options to save and load the game. Javascript will enable all buttons to work by adding functions that handle click events for each button. Additional JS logic will be in-place to handle the mathematics for the game.

Content

Home page

Welcome to Brownie Clicker! An idle game experience where you aim to produce as many brownies as possible. This game is inspired by the classic cookie clicker, and is designed to be easy to pick up. At the heart of the game is a giant brownie. Click the brownie to earn brownie points. Use your brownies to hire workers who will bake you more brownies automatically! As you progress, you will unlock new types of workers, funny in-game news snippets, and other surprises. The more you play, the more brownies you get!
