Brownie Clicker

Welcome to Brownie Clicker! An idle game experience where you aim to produce as many brownies as possible. This game is inspired by the classic cookie clicker game, and is designed to be easy to pick up, and hard to put down.

Instructions:

At the heart of the game is a giant brownie. Click the brownie to earn brownie points. Use your brownie points to hire workers who will bake you more brownies. As you progress, you will unlock new types of workers, funny in-game news snippets, and other surprises. The more you play, the more brownies you get!
